ALKA Productions is currently working on a game that consists of racing and blowing up competitors. The game will have two modes: Racing and Demolition, both in which you can blow your opponents cars up. Racing mode you will race to the finnish against the AI while they try to shoot you with rockets and traps or vice versa! Demolition mode is an arena where your only objective is to destroy the AI with the same tools.
