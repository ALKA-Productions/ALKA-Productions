About ALKA Productions:

ALKA Productions is a two man indie Game Development team that makes games for your entertainment. We use the Unity Engine, Blender and Sketch Up to make our 3D games. Our goal is to become a large company with an award winning franchise. Support of any kind is very much appreciated and will go a long way to our Big Goal.

If you want to keep up to date with ALKA game productions Subscribe to our monthly newsletter: alkagameproductions@newsletter.com

The Group

ALKA Productions is a small group of two people who thoroughly enjoy the programming side of game development. Often in our games there will be more functionality, less bugs and graphics to the best of our ability.
